### Integration Findings

**INT-01 (Moderate):** `LLMProviderRegistryService.InitializeAsync()` not called from any hosted startup service. Providers appear empty in EditorConfigSidebar and SedimentationService until user visits `/settings`. Self-heals once Settings is visited. Affects LLMN-01, LLMN-02, LLMN-03, LIVM-01, PROV-10. Not a crash — functional ordering dependency only.

**INT-02 (Low, Known Deferred):** `LLMModelInfo` has no `IsEnabled` field. Model-level disabled rendering absent in EditorConfigSidebar. Provider-level disable works correctly. Documented as intentionally deferred in Phase 51-02.
